Investment Banking Essentials

This program offers an intensive dive into the core functions of investment banking. You will learn about mergers & acquisitions (M&A), initial public offerings (IPOs), debt and equity capital markets, and advanced valuation techniques. The curriculum includes case studies, financial modeling exercises, and guest lectures from industry veterans.

What You'll Learn:
  • M&A Deal Lifecycle & Structuring
  • Equity & Debt Capital Markets
  • Advanced Financial Modeling & Valuation
  • Regulatory Frameworks in Investment Banking

FinTech Fundamentals & Blockchain

Discover how technology is reshaping the financial industry. This course covers the basics of financial technology, including digital payments, peer-to-peer lending, robo-advisors, and the foundational principles of blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies. Gain insights into the future of finance and the innovations driving it.

Key Topics:
  • Overview of FinTech Ecosystem
  • Blockchain & Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT)
  • Cryptocurrencies and Digital Assets
  • AI & Machine Learning in Finance

Advanced Portfolio Management

Designed for aspiring fund managers and financial advisors, this course provides sophisticated techniques for constructing, optimizing, and managing investment portfolios. Topics include modern portfolio theory, risk management, asset allocation strategies, and performance attribution. You'll work with real market data and simulation tools.

Course Highlights:
  • Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT)
  • Quantitative Risk Management
  • Behavioral Finance in Portfolio Decisions
  • Alternative Investments
