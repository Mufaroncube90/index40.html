Financial Modeling & Valuation

Course Overview:

This course provides a comprehensive understanding of financial modeling, covering discounted cash flow (DCF), leveraged buyout (LBO), and merger & acquisition (M&A) models. You will learn to build dynamic financial models from scratch using Excel and apply them to real-world scenarios.

Key Learning Outcomes:
  • Master Excel functions for financial analysis.
  • Construct integrated financial statements.
  • Perform company valuations using various methodologies.
  • Build LBO and M&A models.
Enroll in this Course

Investment Banking Fundamentals

Course Overview:

An intensive program covering the essentials of investment banking. Topics include equity and debt capital markets, mergers and acquisitions, and an overview of the regulatory environment. Case studies of landmark deals will be analyzed.

Key Learning Outcomes:
  • Understand the role of investment banks.
  • Learn about different types of financial instruments.
  • Analyze M&A transactions.
  • Develop pitch book creation skills.
Enroll in this Course

FinTech & Digital Finance

Course Overview:

This course explores the impact of technology on finance, including blockchain, cryptocurrencies, artificial intelligence, and machine learning applications. Understand how these innovations are reshaping financial services.

Key Learning Outcomes:
  • Assess the impact of blockchain on finance.
  • Understand AI/ML applications in financial services.
  • Explore regulatory challenges in FinTech.
  • Identify emerging opportunities in digital finance.
