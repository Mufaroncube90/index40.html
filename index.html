About the School of Entrepreneurship

Fostering the next generation of visionary founders and business leaders.

The School of Entrepreneurship is designed to empower aspiring and early-stage entrepreneurs with the mindset, skills, and tools necessary to build and scale successful businesses. Our programs are built on a foundation of lean startup methodologies, design thinking, and real-world application.

Our "teaching philosophy" is rooted in experiential learning, mentorship, and peer collaboration. We believe that the best way to learn entrepreneurship is by doing, iterating, and getting direct feedback from the market and experienced mentors. We provide a supportive ecosystem for you to test, fail fast, and grow.

Target Audience:

Individuals with new business ideas, aspiring startup founders, small business owners, and intrapreneurs within larger organizations.

Key Differentiators:

Practical, hands-on projects, direct access to a network of investors and mentors, and a curriculum focused on actionable strategies.
