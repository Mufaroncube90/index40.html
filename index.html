Corporate Finance Fundamentals

Understand capital budgeting, working capital management, and financial statement analysis for businesses.

Corporate Finance Fundamentals - Detailed Information

This course provides a foundational understanding of corporate finance principles, essential for anyone involved in business decision-making. You'll explore topics such as capital budgeting (evaluating investment projects), working capital management (optimizing current assets and liabilities), and in-depth financial statement analysis. We delve into how companies make financing decisions, manage risk, and create value for shareholders. Ideal for business students, entrepreneurs, and finance professionals looking to solidify their understanding of corporate financial strategy.

Curriculum Outline
  • Module 1: Introduction to Corporate Finance & Goals of the Firm
  • Module 2: Time Value of Money
  • Module 3: Capital Budgeting Techniques (NPV, IRR)
  • Module 4: Working Capital Management (Inventory, Accounts Receivable/Payable)
  • Module 5: Sources of Long-Term Finance (Debt, Equity)
  • Module 6: Cost of Capital & Capital Structure
  • Module 7: Financial Statement Analysis (Ratio Analysis)
  • Module 8: Risk Management in Corporate Finance
Key Learning Outcomes
  • Evaluate investment projects using capital budgeting techniques.
  • Manage working capital effectively to optimize liquidity and profitability.
  • Understand different sources of corporate financing and their costs.
  • Analyze financial statements to assess a company's performance.
  • Grasp the relationship between risk and return in corporate decision-making.
  • Apply corporate finance principles to real-world business scenarios.
Target Audience

Business students, aspiring financial managers, small business owners, and professionals in accounting or related fields who need a strong grasp of corporate financial concepts.

Course Format

The course includes video lectures, practical exercises, case studies, and quizzes. It is designed to be self-paced, allowing you to learn at your own convenience, with optional live Q&A sessions.
